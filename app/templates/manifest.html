Use JSON for durable fields like `adjacent_genres`, `adjacent_themes`, `explicit_feedback`, `profile_exclusions`, and `operator_notes`.

Core watch-derived and TMDb-derived fields like `top_keywords`, `favorite_people`, and `favorite_collections` can be tuned manually, but they will usually be refreshed by Profile Architect from live history plus metadata enrichment.
